Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

What data we collect

With your authorization, Loadvion retrieves strength workout activity data only from your Garmin Connect account. This includes:

  • Exercise name
  • Sets, repetitions, and weight used per set
  • Activity date

We also collect your email address for account access.

How we use this data

Activity data is used solely to analyze your historical workouts and propose appropriate working weights for future sessions inside Loadvion. We do not use this data for advertising, profiling, or resale.

Data sharing

We do not sell your activity data. We share data only with service providers (such as secure hosting) strictly to operate the Loadvion service, or if required by law.

Your control

You may revoke Garmin access at any time through your Garmin account settings. You may request a copy of your stored data or request deletion of your data by contacting us. Requests are completed within 30 days.
